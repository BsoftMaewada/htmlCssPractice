The Story in the Book

Chapter 1

Molly had been staring out of her window for like an hour now. On he desk, lying between the copies of Nature, New Scientist, and all the other scientific journals her work had appear in, was a well thumbed copy of On The Road . It has been Molly's favourite book since college, and the longer she spent in these four walls the more she felt she needed to be free.

She had spent the last ten years in this room, sitting under a poster with an Oscar Wilde qoute proclaining the Work is the refuge of people who have nothing better to do. Althought many consider her pioneering work, unravelling the secrets of Ilama DNA, to be an outstanding achievement, Molly did think she had something better to do.
